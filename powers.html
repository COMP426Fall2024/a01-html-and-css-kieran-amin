Introduction

Poseidon is one of the twelve Olympian gods in Greek mythology. He is the god of the sea, earthquakes, and horses.

Powers and Abilities

  • Control over the sea and all its creatures
  • Ability to create storms and calm waters
  • Power to cause earthquakes and shape the land
  • Control over horses and the ability to create new ones
